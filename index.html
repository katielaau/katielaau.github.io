ABOUT
Bay Area Boba (or BAB) is a website made by a passionate boba-lover who wanted to share her vast amounts of boba knowledge with the world. Whether boba is your life or you just want to give it try, Bay Area Boba will help you find the right place with the perfect drink to suit your needs. From reviews to top drinks, everything you will ever need to know about a bay area boba shop can be found here, so make sure to check out the Let's Drink page to find out more!

Boba Guys is a business founded by two SF locals, Bin and Andrew, whose goals were to fundamentally change the way people view boba, tea, and perhaps, food. Using only high quality teas and ingredients, they began to craft boba drinks and have opened four different shops around San Francisco! Quality, passion, and transparency...
TIPS
If you're having trouble finding the place, look for the store with the long line and that will most likely be it! Expect lines - especially on a hot day - so try to go early and leave an ample amount of time for your boba runs! Try their strawberry matcha latte with boba; the combinations of flavors is different... but the good kind of different. :-)
